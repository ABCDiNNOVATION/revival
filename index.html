HI VISITOR,

Are you looking for a place to worship that preach the true word of God? Are you looking for a gathering which is guided by the Holy Spirit? As it is written in Acts 1:8 we welcome you to receive power from Holy Spirit. And you will be His witnesses, telling people about Jesus everywhere- and to the ends of the earth. Revival international Church of God was created in the believe that the Bible is the inspired Word of God. Every Sunday we offer a variety of Malayalam and English music’s. And Inspirational message based on Scripture given by the Holy Spirit. We celebrate the Holy Communion in the 1 st and 3 rd Sunday of every month. Our church gives Sunday school and bible classes to kids and Adults, as a new growing church, we give much importance to our Youth. Every Sunday our ministers offer a very detailed Bible Class.
We encourage you to join our worship service teams, choir, ministers, or other committees that
interest you. You do not need to be a member in order to participate. We are excited about the growth of this new church and would like for you to become a part of our church family. If you are interested in becoming a member of Revival International church of God, Join with us
